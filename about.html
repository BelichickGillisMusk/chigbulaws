Your Elk Grove Attorney

Clifford Chigbu, the founder of Chigbu Law, is not only an experienced attorney but also an immigrant himself. He understands the unique challenges faced by our diverse community and uses his in-depth knowledge of local laws and courts to craft personalized legal strategies for each client.

Having navigated the complexities of the immigration system firsthand, Clifford brings unique insight and empathy to every case. His personal journey fuels his commitment to achieving positive outcomes for clients facing all types of legal challenges — from family law to business disputes.

Our Commitment

At Clifford Chigbu Attorney at Law, we are dedicated to providing expert legal representation to individuals and families throughout Elk Grove and the greater Sacramento area. Our firm combines deep legal expertise with a genuine commitment to our clients' well-being.

We believe that every client deserves clear communication, honest counsel, and aggressive advocacy. Whether you're facing a divorce, navigating immigration, starting a business, or recovering from an accident — we are in your corner.

Practice Areas

We offer comprehensive legal services across Family Law, Immigration Law, Business Law, Bankruptcy Law, Auto Accident Law, Personal Injury Law, and Estate Planning. Our client-focused approach ensures tailored strategies to protect your rights and achieve the best outcomes.
