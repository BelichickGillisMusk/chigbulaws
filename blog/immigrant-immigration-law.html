From One Immigrant to Another: Navigating Immigration Law with Clifford Chigbu

← Back to Legal Blog

The journey of an immigrant is paved with both hope and daunting challenges. I know this firsthand — not just as an attorney, but as someone who has lived it.

My Story

When I came to the United States, I faced the same uncertainty, the same bureaucratic complexity, and the same longing for a better life that my clients describe every day. That experience didn't just shape who I am — it defines how I practice law. When you sit across from me, I am not just your attorney. I am someone who understands what is at stake.

What I've Learned

Immigration law is one of the most complex areas of American jurisprudence. It changes frequently, it varies by individual circumstance, and the consequences of mistakes can be life-altering — deportation, family separation, loss of work authorization. This is why having an experienced, empathetic immigration attorney is not optional. It is essential.

How Chigbu Law Can Help

We handle a full range of immigration matters including:

  • Family-based green cards and petitions
  • Naturalization and citizenship applications
  • Work visas (H-1B, L-1, O-1, and more)
  • DACA renewals and related matters
  • Deportation defense and removal proceedings
  • Asylum applications
